<p><p>Hybrid imaging combines two imaging modalities to provide enhanced diagnostic capabilities. The PET/CT market integrates positron emission tomography with computed tomography, offering detailed functional and anatomical information, vital for oncology. Similarly, SPECT/CT merges single-photon emission computed tomography with CT, enabling precise localization of functional abnormalities. The PET/MRI market pairs positron emission tomography with magnetic resonance imaging, delivering superior soft tissue contrast and advanced functional imaging, making it particularly beneficial in neurology and oncology. Each combination enhances diagnostic accuracy and patient management.</p></p>
