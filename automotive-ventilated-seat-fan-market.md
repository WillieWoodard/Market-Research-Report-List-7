<p><strong>The Automotive Ventilated Seat Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Radial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ventilated seat fan market primarily features two fan types: axial fans and radial fans. Axial fans facilitate airflow parallel to the fan axis, providing efficient ventilation with lower noise levels, making them ideal for passenger comfort. In contrast, radial fans, with a design that allows air to flow perpendicular to the axis, deliver higher pressure and can effectively circulate air within tightly packed spaces. Both types enhance seat comfort by regulating temperature and moisture, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
