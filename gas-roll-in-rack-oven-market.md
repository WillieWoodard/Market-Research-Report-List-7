<p><p>The gas roll-in rack oven market includes two main types: static roll-in rack ovens and rotating roll-in rack ovens. Static roll-in ovens feature a fixed position for racks, allowing consistent baking of multiple trays but may have limited heat distribution. In contrast, rotating roll-in rack ovens facilitate even cooking by rotating trays within the oven, enhancing airflow and heat distribution. Both types are prized for their efficiency and capacity, catering primarily to bakeries and food production facilities.</p></p>
