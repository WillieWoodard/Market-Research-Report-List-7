<p><ul><li>Pure Electronic</li><li>Backup Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Steer-by-Wire System market includes two main types: Pure Electronic and Backup Mechanical systems. Pure Electronic systems eliminate mechanical linkages, relying entirely on electronic sensors and actuators for steering, offering enhanced precision, flexibility, and design freedom. Backup Mechanical systems, while primarily electronic, retain mechanical components as a safety redundancy in case of electronic failure. Both types aim to improve vehicle dynamics, reduce weight, and enable advanced driver-assistance features, with Backup Mechanical systems providing an added layer of reliability for critical safety scenarios.</p></p>
