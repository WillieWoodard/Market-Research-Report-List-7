<p><p>The Neonatal Intensive Care Respiratory Devices market includes several key types essential for supporting premature and critically ill infants. Nebulizers deliver medication directly to the lungs, while inhalers facilitate medication inhalation. Ventilators assist with breathing when infants cannot do it independently. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices maintain open airways by providing a constant flow of air. Apnea monitors track an infant's breathing patterns, alerting caregivers to potential respiratory distress, ensuring comprehensive respiratory care in neonatal settings.</p></p>
