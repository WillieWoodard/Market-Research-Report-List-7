<p><p>The Blood Glucose Monitoring System market is primarily divided into two types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices. SMBG devices, such as traditional glucose meters, allow individuals to check their blood sugar levels at specific times, providing point-in-time readings. In contrast, CGM devices offer continuous tracking of glucose levels throughout the day and night, delivering real-time data and trends, which help users make informed decisions about insulin dosing and lifestyle adjustments.</p></p>
