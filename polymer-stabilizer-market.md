<p><p>The polymer stabilizer market includes three main types: antioxidants, heat stabilizers, and light stabilizers. Antioxidants prevent oxidative degradation, enhancing the longevity of polymers. Heat stabilizers protect against thermal degradation during processing and usage, ensuring material integrity at elevated temperatures. Light stabilizers absorb or filter harmful UV radiation, preventing photodegradation and maintaining color and mechanical properties. Together, these stabilizers play a critical role in improving the performance and durability of polymer products across various industries.</p></p>
