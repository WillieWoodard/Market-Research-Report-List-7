<p><p>The lubricant packaging market includes various types designed for versatility and efficiency. Stand-up pouches offer lightweight, space-saving solutions. Bottles are common for consumer and automotive lubricants, while drums are ideal for bulk storage. Pails are used for larger quantities, and cans provide rigid protection. Tubes allow for easy dispensing of smaller amounts. Kegs are suitable for commercial use, while bag-in-box systems offer convenient transportation. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) support large-scale operations, ensuring effective handling and storage.</p></p>
