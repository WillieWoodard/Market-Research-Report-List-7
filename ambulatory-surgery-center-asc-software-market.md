<p><p>The Ambulatory Surgery Center (ASC) software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ambulatory software offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing healthcare providers to access patient data and manage operations remotely, which is ideal for multi-location practices. On-premise ambulatory software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the facility's servers, providing enhanced control over data security and customization but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to specific operational needs of ASCs.</p></p>
