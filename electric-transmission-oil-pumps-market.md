<p><p>The Electric Transmission Oil Pumps market consists of two main types: integrated pumps and separate pumps. Integrated pumps are designed as a single unit combined with the transmission system, offering compactness and efficiency while reducing installation complexity. Separate pumps, on the other hand, function independently from the transmission, allowing more flexibility in design and placement. Both types serve to circulate oil within the transmission, ensuring proper lubrication and cooling, thereby enhancing operational reliability and performance in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
