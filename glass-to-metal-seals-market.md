<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-to-metal seals market primarily features two main types: matched seals and compression seals. Matched seals consist of glass and metal components that are precisely engineered to fit together, ensuring a reliable bond and minimal thermal expansion issues. In contrast, compression seals utilize a tighter fit between the glass and metal, creating pressure that enhances the seal's integrity. Both types serve critical functions in electronic devices, lighting, and vacuum systems, providing durability and protection from contaminants.</p></p>
