<p><p>The Odd Layers Cross Laminated Timber (CLT) market is categorized into two main types: adhesive-bonded and mechanically fastened systems. Adhesive-bonded CLT involves using strong adhesives to bond layers of wood at right angles, creating a solid, stable structure ideal for various applications. Mechanically fastened CLT relies on screws, nails, or other fasteners to hold the layers together, offering a different structural aesthetic and potentially easier disassembly. Both systems provide unique benefits for construction, depending on design requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
