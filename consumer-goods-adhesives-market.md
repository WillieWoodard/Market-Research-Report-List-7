<p><p>The consumer goods adhesives market is categorized into one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready for application, suitable for quick bonding tasks in home repairs and crafts. They typically include products like hot glue and single-package epoxies. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before use, offering stronger bonds and enhanced durability for demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, from DIY projects to professional uses.</p></p>
