<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Braking Systems market includes various advanced technologies designed to enhance vehicle safety and control. **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)** prevents wheel lockup during braking, ensuring stability. **EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution)** optimizes brake force between wheels for better balance. **ASR (Traction Control System)** prevents wheel spin during acceleration. **ESP (Electronic Stability Program)** maintains vehicle direction during skids. **EPB (Electronic Parking Brake)** replaces manual handbrakes with automated systems. Other systems include adaptive braking and collision avoidance technologies, collectively improving safety, efficiency, and driving performance.</p></p>
