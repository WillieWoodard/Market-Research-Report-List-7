<p><p>The cast polyurethane prepolymer market is primarily categorized into polyester-based and polyether-based types. Polyester-based prepolymers offer excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications requiring durability, such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, polyether-based prepolymers are known for their flexibility and low-temperature performance, making them ideal for soft applications like foams and elastomers. Each type serves distinct industry needs, from automotive and construction to footwear and furniture, depending on performance characteristics and environmental conditions.</p></p>
