<p><p>The RF cable assemblies market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Semi-Rigid RF Cable Assemblies** are characterized by their solid inner conductor and rigid outer shield, offering excellent performance in high-frequency applications due to minimal signal loss and susceptibility to interference.</p><p>2. **Semi-Flexible RF Cable Assemblies** balance rigidity and flexibility, allowing for easier routing in constrained spaces while maintaining good signal integrity.</p><p>3. **Flexible RF Cable Assemblies** are designed for maximum flexibility, making them suitable for dynamic environments where frequent movement or bending is required without compromising performance.</p></p>
