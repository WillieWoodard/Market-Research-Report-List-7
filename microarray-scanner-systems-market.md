<p><p>The microarray scanner systems market includes various types based on detection methods. Fluorescence microarray scanners utilize fluorescent labels to detect hybridization signals, offering high sensitivity and multiplexing capabilities. Charge-coupled devices (CCD) microarray scanners capture images of the labeled arrays using CCD technology, providing high-resolution data. Colorimetric microarray scanners rely on colorimetric assays to visualize results, making them simpler and cost-effective. Additionally, other scanner types may incorporate emerging technologies and hybrid approaches to enhance performance and versatility in gene expression analysis.</p></p>
