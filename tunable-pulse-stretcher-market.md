<p><p>The Tunable Pulse Stretcher market encompasses two main types: Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) and Chirped Fiber Bragg Grating (CFBG). FBG utilizes reflective properties of the grating to manage light pulses, allowing for precise control in optical communications and signal processing. CFBG, on the other hand, features a varying grating period, enabling the stretching of pulses over a broader range of wavelengths. Both technologies are essential in enhancing performance in applications like telecommunications, laser systems, and optical sensing.</p></p>
