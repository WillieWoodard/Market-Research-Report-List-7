Temperature sensors monitor component heat to prevent overheating. Level sensors measure fluid levels in hydraulic systems, while pressure sensors ensure optimal hydraulic and pneumatic operation. Position sensors track the turbine's orientation, facilitating precise adjustments for maximizing energy capture based on wind direction.</p></p>
