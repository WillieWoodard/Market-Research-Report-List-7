<p><p>The polypropylene wax market is divided into two main types: normal PP wax and modified PP wax. Normal PP wax is a high molecular weight polymer known for its excellent thermal stability and lubricating properties, making it suitable for various applications such as coatings, plastics, and adhesives. Modified PP wax, on the other hand, has undergone chemical alterations to enhance its performance attributes, offering improved compatibility, adhesion, and dispersibility. Both types serve diverse sectors, including automotive, packaging, and construction.</p></p>
