<p><strong>The CMOS Image Sensor CIS for Medical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Shutter</li><li>Global Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS Image Sensors (CIS) are pivotal in the medical market, particularly for imaging applications. Rolling shutter sensors capture images line by line, making them suitable for non-moving subjects but can distort fast-moving ones. Conversely, global shutter sensors capture the entire frame simultaneously, minimizing motion artifacts and ensuring precise imaging of dynamic scenes, essential in medical diagnostics. The choice between these types depends on the specific imaging requirements, such as speed, accuracy, and the nature of the medical observation.</p></p>
