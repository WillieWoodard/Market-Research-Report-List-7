<p><p>Particle foam molding machines are categorized into manual and automatic types based on their operation. Manual machines require human intervention for loading materials, monitoring the process, and handling finished products, making them suitable for small-scale production or specialized applications. In contrast, automatic machines are fully automated, featuring advanced controls and robotics for efficient production with minimal human oversight. They are ideal for large-scale operations, offering higher throughput, consistency, and reduced labor costs, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
