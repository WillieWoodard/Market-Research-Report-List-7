<p><strong>The Lead-Acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead-acid battery market is primarily divided into two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and designed to prevent electrolyte spillage, making them ideal for applications requiring reliability and minimal service. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, are vented and require regular maintenance, offering high power and durability for larger applications like automotive and industrial uses. Both types serve diverse sectors, including automotive, renewable energy, and backup power systems.</p></p>
