<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MIM (Metal Injection Molding) injection equipment market is categorized into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal injection molding machines are widely used for their ease of access, allowing for larger molds and higher production rates, making them ideal for mass production. In contrast, vertical injection machines offer advantages in a compact footprint and are suited for insert molding processes, where parts are assembled within the mold during injection. Both types serve specific manufacturing needs, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
