<p><p>The low platinum catalyst market features three main types: Pt-based alloy catalysts, which combine platinum with other metals to enhance activity while reducing platinum use; Pt-based core-shell catalysts, where a core material is coated with a thin layer of platinum, optimizing performance and efficiency; and Pt single atom catalysts, where individual platinum atoms are dispersed on a support, maximizing surface area and catalytic effectiveness. Each type aims to lower platinum consumption while maintaining high catalytic performance in various applications.</p></p>
