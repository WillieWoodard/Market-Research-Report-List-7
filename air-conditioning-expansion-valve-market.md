<p><p>The air conditioning expansion valve market includes various types, primarily the Thermal Expansion Valve (TXV) and the Thermostatic Expansion Valve. The TXV regulates refrigerant flow based on temperature and pressure, ensuring optimal cooling efficiency. It adjusts the flow rate in response to the evaporator's needs, enhancing system performance. Conversely, the Thermostatic Expansion Valve includes a sensing bulb that reacts to temperature changes, allowing for precise control and maintaining consistent evaporator pressure, which improves energy efficiency and overall system reliability.</p></p>
