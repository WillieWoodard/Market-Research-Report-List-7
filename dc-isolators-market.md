<p><p>The DC isolators market can be categorized into three main types: Single Break Isolators, Double Break Isolators, and Pantograph Type Isolators. Single Break Isolators provide a simple and cost-effective solution for disconnecting circuits, ideal for lower power applications. Double Break Isolators offer enhanced safety with two breaking points, making them suitable for high-voltage operations. Pantograph Type Isolators are designed for overhead applications, typically in transportation systems, allowing for flexible connectivity while maintaining reliable disconnection in dynamic environments.</p></p>
