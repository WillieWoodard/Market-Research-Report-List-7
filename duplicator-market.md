<p><ul><li>Mechanical Duplicators</li><li>Digital Duplicators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Duplicator market types are primarily categorized into mechanical and digital duplicators. Mechanical duplicators use a stencil-based process to produce multiple copies, making them ideal for high-volume printing with lower setup costs but limited capabilities regarding color and detail. In contrast, digital duplicators leverage advanced printing technology, allowing for high-quality, color-rich outputs and more versatile printing options. Digital duplicators are more efficient for smaller runs and on-demand printing, catering to a diverse range of business needs.</p></p>
