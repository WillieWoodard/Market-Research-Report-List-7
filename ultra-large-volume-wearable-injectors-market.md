<p><p>The ultra-large volume wearable injectors market is categorized into on-body and off-body injectors. In contrast, off-body injectors are separate devices that can be positioned close to the injection site but are not adhered to the body. They provide flexibility and can be used in various settings, complementing patient convenience and treatment options.</p></p>
