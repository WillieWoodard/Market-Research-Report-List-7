<p><p>The outdoor mist and fog devices market is divided into two main types: low-pressure and high-pressure devices. Low-pressure devices use water pressure typically below 100 psi, creating a gentle mist suitable for cooling outdoor spaces and gardens. In contrast, high-pressure devices operate above 1000 psi, producing finer mist droplets for more effective cooling and evaporative cooling applications. High-pressure systems are often employed in commercial settings, while low-pressure units are popular for residential use, enhancing outdoor comfort and aesthetics.</p></p>
