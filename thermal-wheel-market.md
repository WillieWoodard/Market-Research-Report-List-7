<p><p>A thermal wheel, also known as an enthalpy wheel, is a highly efficient heat recovery device used in HVAC systems to transfer thermal and latent energy between outgoing and incoming air streams. This technology enhances energy efficiency by pre-conditioning incoming air, contributing to reduced energy consumption and lower operating costs.</p><p>The Thermal Wheel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.40% during the forecast period, driven by increasing energy efficiency regulations, a greater emphasis on sustainable building practices, and rising demand for advanced HVAC solutions. Additionally, the growing adoption of these systems in various sectors, including healthcare, data centers, and hospitality, is fostering market expansion.
