<p><strong>The Anion Exchange Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strong Base Anion Resin</li><li>Weak Base Anion Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anion exchange resin market comprises two primary types: strong base anion resin and weak base anion resin. Strong base anion resins efficiently remove a wide range of anions, including carbonate and sulfate, by using quaternary ammonium groups. They are ideal for applications requiring deionization and water purification. In contrast, weak base anion resins primarily target specific anions like nitrate and phosphate, functioning effectively in lower pH environments. Both types play crucial roles in water treatment, chemical processing, and more.</p></p>
