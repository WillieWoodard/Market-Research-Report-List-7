<p><p>Polypropylene woven bags and sacks are popular packaging solutions made from woven polypropylene fibers. The market is divided into two main types: laminated and non-laminated. Laminated polypropylene woven bags have an additional layer of material, providing enhanced strength, moisture resistance, and a smoother surface for printing, making them ideal for premium products. Non-laminated polypropylene woven bags, on the other hand, are more cost-effective and suitable for bulk goods, offering durability and breathability without the added features of lamination.</p></p>
