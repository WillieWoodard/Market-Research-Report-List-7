<p><strong>The Screw Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Injected Compressors</li><li>Oil-Free Compressors</li><li>Liquid Injection Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw compressors are categorized into three main types based on lubrication and operation: </p><p>1. **Oil-Injected Compressors** utilize oil for internal lubrication, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear, making them suitable for industrial applications with high demands.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Oil-Free Compressors** operate without oil, providing clean air output, ideal for sensitive applications like food processing or pharmaceuticals.</p><p>3. **Liquid Injection Compressors** employ a liquid cooling medium to maintain optimal temperature and pressure, offering a balance between oil-injected and oil-free operations for various industrial needs.</p></p>
