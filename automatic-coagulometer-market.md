<p><p>The automatic coagulometer market consists of several detection types, including mechanical, photo-optical, electrochemical, and others. Mechanical detection involves measuring changes in viscosity or impedance during coagulation. Photo-optical detection utilizes light transmission or absorbance changes to assess clot formation. Electrochemical detection measures electrical signals generated during coagulation processes. Other methods may include advanced techniques combining multiple detection principles. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse clinical and laboratory needs for effective coagulation testing and monitoring.</p></p>
