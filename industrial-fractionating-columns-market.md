<p><p>The industrial fractionating columns market encompasses two primary processes: distillation and extraction. Distillation involves the separation of mixtures based on differences in boiling points, enabling the purification of liquids and the recovery of valuable components from feedstocks. In contrast, extraction focuses on isolating specific compounds from a mixture, often using solvents to selectively dissolve desired substances. Both processes are integral to industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food, optimizing the production and quality of various products while enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
