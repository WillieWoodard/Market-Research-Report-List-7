<p><p>Coated fine paper is categorized into gloss-coated and dull-coated paper, serving distinct market needs. Gloss-coated paper features a shiny finish, enhancing color vibrancy and image clarity, making it ideal for high-quality prints, magazines, and promotional materials. In contrast, dull-coated paper has a matte finish that reduces glare, providing a more subdued appearance, suitable for text-heavy documents, brochures, and books where readability is prioritized. Both types cater to specific audiences and preferences in the printing and publishing industries.</p></p>
