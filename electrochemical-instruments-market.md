<p><p>The electrochemical instruments market encompasses various techniques used for analyzing chemical properties through electrical signals. Potentiometry measures the voltage of electrochemical cells to determine ion concentration, while voltammetry assesses current changes to study redox reactions. Coulometry quantifies chemical species by measuring total electrical charge passed during a reaction. Other methods may include impedimetric and conductometric techniques, expanding the range of applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and food safety, thus contributing to market growth.</p></p>
