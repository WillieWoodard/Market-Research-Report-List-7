<p><p>The handbike market comprises three main types: manual, electric, and hybrid. Manual handbikes rely solely on the user's physical strength, promoting fitness and independence. Electric handbikes incorporate a battery-powered motor to assist with pedaling, making them ideal for longer distances or users with limited strength. Hybrid models blend both manual and electric features, allowing riders to switch between modes based on their needs, providing versatility for varied terrains and user preferences. Each type caters to different rider requirements and lifestyles.</p></p>
