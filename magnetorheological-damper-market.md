<p><ul><li>Active Damper</li><li>Semi Active Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetorheological (MR) damper market is primarily categorized into active and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize external energy to control the damping force continuously, adapting quickly to changing conditions for optimal performance. In contrast, semi-active dampers employ a variable damping mechanism, adjusting the fluid's viscosity through a magnetic field, but do not require external power for operation. Both types enhance vehicle stability and comfort, with applications in automotive, aerospace, and civil engineering enhancing their market growth.</p></p>
