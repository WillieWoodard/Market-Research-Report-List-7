<p><p>The compression stockings market consists of various types, primarily gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks provide graduated compression, offering increased pressure at the ankle and gradually decreasing pressure up the leg, promoting blood circulation and reducing swelling. In contrast, anti-embolism socks are designed specifically for immobile patients, preventing deep vein thrombosis (DVT) during surgeries or prolonged bed rest. Both types serve critical medical purposes, enhancing patient recovery and comfort while addressing specific vascular health needs.</p></p>
