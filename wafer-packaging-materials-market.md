<p><p>The wafer packaging materials market encompasses various components essential for semiconductor packaging. Lead frames provide the structural framework for connecting chips to external circuitry. Package substrates serve as the foundation for mounting and interconnecting die, enhancing thermal and electrical performance. Ceramic packaging materials offer durability and heat resistance for high-performance applications. Bonding wires connect the die to the package, while die bonding materials adhere chips to substrates. Together, these elements ensure the reliability and efficiency of electronic devices.</p></p>
