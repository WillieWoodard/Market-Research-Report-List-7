<p><p>Mandarin oil is categorized into three market types: Absolute, Concentrates, and Blends. **Absolute** refers to the pure, highly concentrated form of mandarin oil, extracted through solvent methods, prized for its intense aroma and therapeutic properties. **Concentrates** are less refined, offering a potent but slightly diluted version, often used in flavoring and fragrances. **Blends** combine mandarin oil with other essential oils or additives, creating versatile products tailored for specific applications like aromatherapy, cosmetics, or cleaning products, balancing cost and functionality.</p></p>
