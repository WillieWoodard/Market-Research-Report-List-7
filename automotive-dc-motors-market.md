<p><strong>The Automotive DC Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>BLDC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive DC motors market includes brushed and brushless (BLDC) types. Brushed DC motors use brushes and a commutator for current direction, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with higher maintenance and lower efficiency. BLDC motors use electronic controllers, eliminating brushes, resulting in higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower maintenance, making them ideal for modern electric vehicles and advanced automotive systems. While brushed motors are still used in basic applications, BLDC motors dominate due to their superior performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
