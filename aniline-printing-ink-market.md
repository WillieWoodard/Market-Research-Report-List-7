<p><p>The aniline printing ink market comprises three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV-curable inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and suitable for various substrates, offering low odor and quick drying times. Solvent-based inks provide excellent adherence and durability, making them ideal for outdoor applications. UV-curable inks use ultraviolet light for rapid curing, resulting in high-quality finishes and vibrant colors, often used in high-speed printing processes. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing performance, environmental concerns, and application requirements.</p></p>
