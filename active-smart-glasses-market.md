<p><p>The active smart glasses market encompasses various technologies for controlling light and privacy. Suspended Particle Device (SPD) glass uses suspended particles to regulate light transmission. Electrochromic glass changes color when voltage is applied, allowing variable opacity. Liquid Crystal/Polymer Disperse Liquid Crystal glass employs liquid crystals to control light flow, while micro-blinds feature tiny, adjustable slats for shading. Nanocrystal glass utilizes nanotechnology to create transparent materials that can switch from clear to opaque, providing versatile sunlight and privacy management solutions.</p></p>
