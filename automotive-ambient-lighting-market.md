<p><p>The automotive ambient lighting market encompasses two primary types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) products and aftermarket products. OEM products are integrated lighting solutions installed by vehicle manufacturers during production, enhancing the aesthetic appeal and functionality of new vehicles. In contrast, aftermarket products are standalone lighting solutions purchased and installed by consumers after the vehicle's purchase, allowing customization and personalization. Both segments cater to the growing demand for enhanced interior experiences, focusing on aesthetics and improved visibility within vehicles.</p></p>
