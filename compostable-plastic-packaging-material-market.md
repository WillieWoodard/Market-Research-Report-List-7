<p><p>The compostable plastic packaging market includes several key types. PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate) is a versatile biodegradable polymer known for flexibility. PBS (Polybutylene Succinate) offers good thermal properties and biodegradability. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is derived from renewable resources and is widely used for films and containers. PHA (Polyhydroxyalkanoates) is produced by microbial fermentation and boasts excellent biodegradability. Starch blends combine starch with other materials to enhance performance. Other types may include various biopolymers and composites, contributing to sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
