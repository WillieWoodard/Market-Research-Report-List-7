<p><p>The vacuum beard trimmer market can be divided into two primary types: external razors and built-in razors. External razors utilize a separate adjustable razor head that captures cut hair in a disposable chamber, enhancing user convenience and cleanliness. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for design and functionality in beard maintenance.</p></p>
