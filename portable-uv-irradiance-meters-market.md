<p><p>The Portable UV Irradiance Meters market consists of three primary types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C irradiance meters. UV-A meters measure longer wavelengths (320-400 nm), primarily used in cosmetic and industrial applications. UV-B meters focus on shorter wavelengths (280-320 nm), crucial for assessing skin damage and UV exposure risk. UV-C meters measure wavelengths (100-280 nm) effective for germicidal applications, widely utilized in disinfection processes. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
