<p><p>Dry scroll pumps are essential in the semiconductor market, providing a clean and reliable vacuum solution. The two primary types are static vortex and moving vortex pumps. Static vortex pumps utilize a fixed design to create a spiral airflow, helping to evacuate gases efficiently without moving parts. In contrast, moving vortex pumps rely on rotating components to generate a vortex that enhances gas transport. Both types offer advantages in terms of low contamination risk and energy efficiency, crucial for semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
