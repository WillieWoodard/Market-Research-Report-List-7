<p><p>The shelf liner market features two primary types: meshed and smooth. Meshed shelf liners are designed with a textured surface that enhances grip, preventing items from slipping and allowing for better airflow around products. They are often used in kitchens and garages. Smooth shelf liners, on the other hand, offer a sleek, easy-to-clean surface, ideal for decorative storage in living spaces or closets. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing functionality with aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
