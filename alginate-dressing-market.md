<p><strong>The Alginate Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flake Dressing</li><li>Strip Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alginate dressings, derived from seaweed, are highly absorbent and used for wound care, especially for exuding wounds. **Flake dressing** consists of loose, fibrous alginate particles that conform well to irregular wound shapes, promoting healing by maintaining a moist environment. **Strip dressing** comes in pre-cut, rectangular forms, ideal for linear or well-defined wounds, offering ease of application and controlled absorption. Both types are biodegradable, non-adherent, and effective in managing moderate to heavy exudate, aiding in wound healing and infection prevention.</p></p>
