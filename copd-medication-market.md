<p><p>The COPD medication market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Short-Acting Bronchodilators** provide quick relief from symptoms by relaxing airway muscles.</p><p>2. **Corticosteroids** reduce inflammation in the airways, improving breathing.</p><p>3. **Methylxanthines** enhance airflow by relaxing bronchial muscles and have mild anti-inflammatory effects.</p><p>4. **Long-Acting Bronchodilators** offer sustained relief for persistent symptoms, aiding daily management.</p><p>5. **Combination Drugs** merge bronchodilators and corticosteroids for comprehensive treatment, addressing multiple aspects of COPD in one formulation.</p></p>
