<p><p>The silt barrier market includes woven and non-woven fibre types. Woven fibre silt barriers are made from tightly interwoven synthetic materials, offering high strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty erosion control and sediment containment. Non-woven fibre barriers, crafted from bonded synthetic fibres, provide flexibility and permeability, suitable for lighter applications like stormwater management. Both types prevent soil erosion and sediment runoff, but woven barriers excel in harsh conditions, while non-woven barriers are preferred for cost-effective, temporary solutions.</p></p>
