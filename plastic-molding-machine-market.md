<p><ul><li>Forming Molding</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion Molding</li><li>Pultrusion Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic molding machine market comprises various types, each serving specific manufacturing needs. Forming molding involves shaping materials using heat and pressure. Injection molding is a widely-used process that injects molten plastic into molds for precise shapes. Extrusion molding continuously shapes material by forcing it through a die, ideal for producing long objects like pipes. Pultrusion molding combines continuous fibers with resin, pulling them through a heated die for strong, lightweight components, often used in construction and automotive applications.</p></p>
