<p><p>The precision ball screw spline market is categorized into linear and rotary types. Linear ball screw splines enable smooth motion along a straight path, making them suitable for applications like CNC machines and robotics where linear positioning is crucial. In contrast, rotary ball screw splines facilitate rotational movement, ideal for mechanisms requiring precise angular motion, such as in servo motors or automated machinery. Both types emphasize high accuracy and efficiency, catering to diverse industrial automation needs.</p></p>
