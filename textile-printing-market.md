<p><p>The **Textile Printing Market** includes **Direct Printing** and **Resist Printing** as key types. **Direct Printing** involves applying color directly onto the fabric using techniques like screen, digital, or block printing, creating vibrant designs. **Resist Printing** uses methods like batik or tie-dye, where a resist agent blocks dye from certain areas, creating patterns. Both methods cater to diverse fashion and home décor needs, offering unique aesthetics and customization. Direct printing is faster, while resist printing provides intricate, artisanal results.</p></p>
