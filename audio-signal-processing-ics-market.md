<p><p>The audio signal processing IC market is divided into two main types: analog signal processing ICs and digital signal processing ICs. Analog signal processing ICs handle continuous audio signals, enabling tasks like amplification and filtering, which are crucial for high-quality sound reproduction in systems such as traditional amplifiers and radios. In contrast, digital signal processing ICs convert audio signals into digital form, allowing sophisticated algorithms for tasks like noise reduction and audio effects, commonly used in modern audio devices and applications.</p></p>
