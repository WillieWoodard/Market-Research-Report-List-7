<p><p>Stoma or ostomy care products primarily include two types of systems: one-piece systems and two-piece systems. One-piece systems integrate the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, simplifying application and removal, making them user-friendly. In contrast, two-piece systems consist of a separate pouch and skin barrier, allowing for more flexibility in changing pouches while maintaining the skin barrier in place for longer periods. Both systems cater to individual preferences, lifestyle needs, and different levels of mobility and activity.</p></p>
