<p><p>Mask blanks are essential components in photolithography, used for semiconductor manufacturing. Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks minimize light reflection, enhancing imaging precision and reducing defects. They are ideal for advanced lithography processes. Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks, on the other hand, manipulate light phase to create sharper images, improving resolution and enabling the production of smaller features on chips. Both types are critical in the production of high-performance semiconductor devices, driving advancements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
