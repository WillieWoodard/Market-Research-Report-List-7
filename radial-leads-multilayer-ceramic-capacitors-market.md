<p><p>Radial Leads Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors are categorized based on dielectric materials and temperature stability. NPO (COG) capacitors offer high stability and low loss, making them suitable for precision applications. X7R capacitors provide moderate stability and capacitance values, commonly used in decoupling and filtering. Y5V capacitors have high capacitance but poor stability, suitable for less critical applications. Z5U capacitors offer moderate capacitance with wide voltage variations, typically used in general-purpose applications. Other types may include specialty formulations for niche uses.</p></p>
