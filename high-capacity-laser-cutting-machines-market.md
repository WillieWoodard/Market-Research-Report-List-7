<p><p>The high-capacity laser cutting machines market includes three key types: solid-state lasers, fiber lasers, and CO2 lasers. Solid-state lasers utilize a solid gain medium, offering high power and efficiency, suitable for various materials. Fiber lasers, known for their compactness and versatility, deliver high-quality cuts with lower operational costs, ideal for metals. CO2 lasers, commonly used for cutting non-metal materials like wood and plastics, provide excellent precision but may be less efficient with metals compared to fiber lasers.</p></p>
