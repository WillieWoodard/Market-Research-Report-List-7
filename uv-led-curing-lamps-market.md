<p><p>The UV LED curing lamps market is categorized into three main types: Surface Light Source Lamps, Line Light Source Lamps, and others. Surface Light Source Lamps emit uniform UV light across a flat surface, ideal for curing coatings on large, flat objects. Line Light Source Lamps provide focused UV light in a narrow beam, suited for curing applications requiring precision, such as printing or narrow surfaces. The "Others" category includes specialized or emerging lamp types that offer unique features for niche applications.</p></p>
