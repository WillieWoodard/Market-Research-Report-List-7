<p><p>A telescopic forklift, also known as a telehandler, is a versatile material handling equipment that combines the capabilities of a forklift with that of a crane. It features a telescopic boom that can extend and retract, allowing it to lift, move, and place materials at various heights and distances. Telescopic forklifts are widely used in construction, agriculture, and industrial sectors due to their ability to navigate rough terrain and perform tasks in confined spaces.</p><p>The Telescopic Forklift Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
