<p><p>The solar reflective window films market is categorized primarily into reflective films and dual reflective films. Reflective films are designed to minimize heat and glare by reflecting sunlight, enhancing energy efficiency in buildings. In contrast, dual reflective films offer a balanced solution by reflecting solar heat while maintaining visibility from the inside. This type reduces glare and preserves natural light without compromising aesthetics. Both film types contribute to improved comfort, reduced cooling costs, and increased privacy in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
