<p><p>The alkyd coatings market is categorized into three main types: non-drying, drying, and semi-drying coatings. Non-drying coatings remain workable without forming a solid film, often used for temporary applications. Drying coatings undergo a chemical reaction upon exposure to air, hardening quickly, making them ideal for industrial and automotive finishes. Semi-drying coatings strike a balance, drying at a moderate pace, which allows for easier application while providing a durable finish. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
