<p><ul><li>Single Acting Steam Valve</li><li>Double Acting Steam Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric steam valves market comprises two main types: single acting steam valves and double acting steam valves. Single acting steam valves operate using a spring return mechanism, enabling them to close with the help of a spring when not energized, facilitating energy efficiency. In contrast, double acting steam valves use electric power for both opening and closing, offering more precise control and faster response times. Both types are crucial for various industrial applications, ensuring safe and efficient steam management.</p></p>
