<p><p>The Paper Phenolic Copper Clad Laminate market consists of two primary types: single-sided and double-sided copper clad laminates. Single-sided copper clad laminates feature a layer of copper on one side, suitable for basic circuit applications, while double-sided laminates have copper layers on both sides, allowing for more complex and dense circuitry. These laminates are widely used in electronics for printed circuit boards (PCBs), providing insulation and mechanical support while facilitating electrical connections.</p></p>
