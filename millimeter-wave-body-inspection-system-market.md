Flat scan technology captures a two-dimensional image of a person, typically used for security screening at entrances. In contrast, cylindrical scan systems create a three-dimensional image by revolving around the subject, providing a more comprehensive view that enhances threat detection capabilities. Both types are utilized in airports, ports, and critical infrastructure to ensure safety and security while maintaining privacy.</p></p>
