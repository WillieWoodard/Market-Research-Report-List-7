<p><p>The Public Transport Smart Card market consists of two main types: contactless smart cards and contact smart cards. Contactless smart cards use RFID technology, allowing users to tap their card on a reader for quick and convenient access to services, enhancing passenger flow and reducing wait times. In contrast, contact smart cards require physical insertion into a reader, offering a more traditional interaction. Both types aim to streamline fare collection and improve the overall efficiency of public transport systems.</p></p>
