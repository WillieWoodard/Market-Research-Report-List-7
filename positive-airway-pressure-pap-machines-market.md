<p><p>The Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) machines market comprises three primary types: Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) machines deliver a steady airflow to keep airways open during sleep, mainly used for obstructive sleep apnea. Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) machines provide two pressure settings: a higher pressure for inhalation and a lower pressure for exhalation, catering to patients with higher respiratory needs. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) machines adjust pressure levels in real-time based on detected changes in breathing patterns, offering a tailored treatment approach.</p></p>
