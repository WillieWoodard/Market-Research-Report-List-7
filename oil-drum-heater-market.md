<p><strong>The Oil Drum Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heating Band Type Heater</li><li>Heating Jacket Type Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil drum heater market primarily features two types: heating band type heaters and heating jacket type heaters. Heating band type heaters wrap around the oil drum, providing direct heat to the surface and efficiently warming the contents. In contrast, heating jacket type heaters encase the entire drum, circulating heated fluid around it for uniform temperature control. Both types are essential for preventing oil from thickening in low temperatures, ensuring optimal fluidity for various industrial applications.</p></p>
