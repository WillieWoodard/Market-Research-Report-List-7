<p><strong>The Quantitative Dispensing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric</li><li>Weight Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quantitative Dispensing Machine market includes two primary types: volumetric and weight-type dispensers. Volumetric dispensers measure liquids based on their volume, using calibrated chambers to ensure precise delivery. They are commonly used in food and beverage industries. Weight-type dispensers, on the other hand, determine the amount of product dispensed by measuring weight, making them ideal for powdered substances and granules. Both types aim to optimize accuracy and efficiency in dispensing, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
