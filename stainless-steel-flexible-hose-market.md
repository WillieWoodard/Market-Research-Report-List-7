<p><p>The stainless steel flexible hose market features two main types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses consist of interlocking metal strips that provide strength and flexibility, making them suitable for applications with high temperature and pressure. In contrast, corrugated hoses have a sinusoidal pattern that enhances their flexibility and allows for better movement, often used in industries requiring gentle handling of fluids. Both types are essential in various applications, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial processes.</p></p>
