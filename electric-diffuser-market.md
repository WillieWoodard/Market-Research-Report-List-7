<p><strong>The Electric Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nebulizing</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Heat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric diffuser market includes three main types: nebulizing, ultrasonic, and heat diffusers. Nebulizing diffusers utilize an air pump to create a fine mist of essential oils, offering potent aromatherapy benefits. Ultrasonic diffusers use ultrasonic vibrations to disperse water and oil, producing a mist while also humidifying the air. Heat diffusers rely on gentle heat to evaporate essential oils, providing a more subtle aroma. Each type caters to different preferences for aroma intensity and air quality enhancement.</p></p>
