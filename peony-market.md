<p><p>Peony market types primarily include Paeonia suffruticosa and Paeonia lactiflora. Conversely, Paeonia lactiflora, or Chinese peony, features herbaceous growth and is popular for its vibrant blooms and use in traditional medicine. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences, with their flowers sought after for landscaping, floral arrangements, and cultural significance, driving demand in global markets.</p></p>
