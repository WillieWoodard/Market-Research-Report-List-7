<p><p>The self-injection devices market comprises three main types: needle-free injectors, pen injectors, and wearable injectors. Needle-free injectors utilize high-pressure technology to deliver medication through the skin without needles, enhancing comfort and convenience. Pen injectors are compact devices designed for ease of use, allowing patients to administer precise doses of medication, often for chronic conditions like diabetes. Wearable injectors are advanced devices that can be attached to the body, delivering medications over extended periods, promoting patient adherence to treatment regimens.</p></p>
