<p><strong>The Crude Desalter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Desalter</li><li>Two-stage Desalter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crude desalter market primarily features two types: single-stage and two-stage desalter systems. A single-stage desalter uses a one-step process to remove impurities from crude oil, typically utilizing electrostatic separation. In contrast, a two-stage desalter employs a sequential approach, where the crude passes through two separate dehydration and desalting stages, enhancing the removal efficiency of salts and water. Both systems aim to optimize crude quality and reduce operational issues in refineries, catering to varying crude oil characteristics and processing needs.</p></p>
