<p><p>The copper foil market for lithium-ion batteries is primarily divided into two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrochemical process, offering high purity and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by physically rolling copper into thin sheets, which can be less expensive but may have variations in thickness and performance. Both types are essential for improving battery efficiency and conductivity in energy storage systems.</p></p>
