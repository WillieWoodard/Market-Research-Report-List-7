Millimeter wave modules operate in the 30 GHz to 300 GHz range, enabling high data rates and short-range communication, essential for applications like 5G. Centimeter wave modules function between 1 GHz and 30 GHz, often used in radar and wireless communication. The "other" category encompasses various frequencies and technologies, catering to specialized applications beyond the primary classifications.</p></p>
