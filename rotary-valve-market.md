<p><p>The rotary valve market encompasses various types, including butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves are widely used for regulating flow, featuring a rotating disc for quick shut-off. Ball valves provide tight sealing with a spherical closure element, ideal for on/off applications. Plug valves utilize a cylindrical or tapered plug to control flow, offering reliable performance in various conditions. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, driven by efficiency, durability, and the need for precise flow control in diverse settings.</p></p>
