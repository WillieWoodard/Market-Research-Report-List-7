<p><strong>The Industrial Inkjet Printer Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printer head market is primarily divided into two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric printer heads utilize electric charges to deform materials, allowing precise droplet control, making them suitable for a variety of inks and materials. In contrast, thermal printer heads function by heating the ink to create vapor bubbles, forcing droplets out. Thermal heads are often favored for their simplicity and lower costs, but they are limited to specific inks. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
