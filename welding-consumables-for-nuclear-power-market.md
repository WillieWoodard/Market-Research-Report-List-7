<p><p>Welding consumables for the nuclear power market include materials essential for various welding techniques. Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) utilizes coated electrodes to protect molten welds from contamination, ensuring strong joints. Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) employs a granular flux to shield the weld pool, providing deep penetration and high deposition rates, ideal for thick materials. Other methods may encompass Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW) and Flux-Cored Arc Welding (FCAW), offering tailored solutions for diverse nuclear components, ensuring structural integrity and safety in this critical sector.</p></p>
