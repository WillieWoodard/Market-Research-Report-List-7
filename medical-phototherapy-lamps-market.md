<p><p>The medical phototherapy lamps market consists of three main types: fluorescent lamps, LED lamps, and tungsten halogen lamps. LED lamps offer energy efficiency, longer life, and targeted wavelengths, making them popular for skin disorders like psoriasis and eczema. Tungsten halogen lamps provide intense, focused light and are often used in clinical settings for specific therapeutic applications. Each type serves unique clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
