Straight leads type capacitors feature two leads extending directly from the capacitor, allowing for easy integration into circuit boards. In contrast, crimped leads type capacitors have leads designed with a crimped profile for enhanced mechanical stability and secure connections. Both types cater to diverse applications, offering reliability and performance in electronic circuits, from consumer electronics to industrial systems.</p></p>
